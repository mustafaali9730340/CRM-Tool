Quick Start Guide

1

Login to Your Account

Use your staff credentials to access the CRM system. If you don't have an account, contact your administrator.

2

Add Clients

Navigate to the Clients tab and click "Add Client" to create new client profiles with their contact information and nationality.

3

Create Cases

Create immigration cases for clients and track them through the workflow stages from initial consultation to completion.

4

Manage Tasks & Documents

Add tasks with due dates, track required documents, and log all client interactions for complete case management.
